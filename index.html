About Us

Exotic Design Inc. is your premier destination for high-end vehicle customization in Georgetown South and the GTA. We specialize in vinyl wrapping, commercial graphics, ceramic coating, paint protection film (PPF), tinting, starlight installations, and paint correction.

Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering exceptional craftsmanship with premium materials, ensuring your car, boat, or aircraft receives the best treatment. Whether you're looking for a sleek new look with a custom wrap, advanced protection with PPF, or an eye-catching commercial branding solution, we have the skills and technology to bring your vision to life.

We serve clients who demand the best in automotive styling and protection, using industry-leading products to guarantee long-lasting, showroom-quality finishes. Our goal is to help you protect and personalize your vehicle with top-tier services tailored to your unique needs.

Visit Exotic Design Inc. today and experience the ultimate in vehicle enhancement and protection. Contact us for a free consultation!
